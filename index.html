Selected Research Experience

Jan 2026 - Present

Large-Scale V1 Cortical Model Migration and Evolutionary Training

Undergraduate Researcher | Beijing, China

Participating in a Tsinghua University-Peking University collaborative brain-inspired modeling project. My work focuses on migrating a large-scale V1 cortical model from TensorFlow to BrainPy, adapting neuronal dynamics and simulation workflows, and supporting the early-stage replacement of BPTT with evolutionary computation.

Cross-Dataset Analysis in a Brain-like Geometric Space

Undergraduate Researcher | Chengdu, China

Projecting neuroimaging datasets including SCZD, ABIDE I, ABIDE II, MDD, and ADNI into a shared geometric space derived from LLM head embeddings and graph-theoretic neuroimaging metrics for cross-dataset comparison.
