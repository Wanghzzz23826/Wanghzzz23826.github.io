Research Experience

Selected Experience

Large-Scale V1 Cortical Model Migration and Evolutionary Training

Undergraduate Researcher | Beijing, China | Jan 2026 - Present

Participating in a Tsinghua University-Peking University collaborative project on brain-inspired modeling. My work focuses on migrating a large-scale V1 cortical model from TensorFlow to BrainPy, adapting neuronal dynamics and simulation workflows, and supporting early-stage replacement of BPTT with evolutionary computation for training.

Cross-Dataset Analysis in a Brain-like Geometric Space

Undergraduate Researcher | Chengdu, China | Nov 2025 - Present

Working on a shared geometric space built from LLM head embeddings and graph-theoretic neuroimaging metrics. Projecting datasets including SCZD, ABIDE I, ABIDE II, MDD, and ADNI into this space for comparative analysis of distribution shifts and deviations from normative controls.

Ru-Yuan Lab, Shanghai Jiao Tong University

Visiting Student | Shanghai, China | Jul 2025 - Aug 2025

Participated in summer research activities in computational visual neuroscience and cognitive learning. Attended group meetings, conducted literature review and presentation on related topics, and assisted as a participant in cognitive psychology experiments.

Computational Protein Engineering of PETase

Undergraduate Researcher | Chengdu, China | Nov 2024 - Sep 2025

Designed and implemented a structure-function analysis pipeline for PETase mutants using AlphaFold3, induced-fit docking, metadynamics-based scoring, and molecular dynamics simulation. Contributed to a submitted manuscript on function-oriented protein engineering of IsPETase.
